Taylor Swift

 

Taylor Alison Swift is an American singer-songwriter known for narrative songwriting, which often centers on her personal life and has received widespread critical praise.

Swift became the world's best-selling music artist in 2019 and has won multiple Grammy Awards. Her albums have ranged across country, pop, indie/folk, and alternative rock.

Her album Midnights and songs like 'Anti-Hero' continue to resonate with global audiences — showcasing her craft in storytelling and production.

She is also widely involved in philanthropy and advocacy work and maintains a highly active fanbase worldwide.
