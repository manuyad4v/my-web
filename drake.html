Drake

 

Aubrey Drake Graham, known professionally as Drake, is a Canadian rapper, singer, songwriter and actor who rose to fame for his introspective lyrics and chart-topping releases.

Drake's career includes multiple hit albums and singles that shaped modern hip hop and R&B — he blends emotional lyricism with crossover production.

He’s a cultural figure with a strong influence on playlists and streaming culture — one of his most popular tracks is "God's Plan".

Drake continues to collaborate with artists worldwide and remains a top-streamed artist globally.
